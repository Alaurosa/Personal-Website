Who Am I?

I am Ashley Kim

I'm Ashley and I absolutely hated my name. In the eyes of Middle School me, Ashley was too boring, too common and I wanted to be unique. I thought that a unique name was associated with a unique personality, but this Ashley Kim is very different from other Ashley Kims. The origin of my name is not only extremely rich, but its associated with a forest of ash trees. Not only do I love trees because they are peaceful, warm, and always there for you to lean on, but I also know that they represent something taken for granted by humans, something I felt that I did for my name. I hope to create video games that incorporate trees in some way or form because trees need a voice too.

To achieve the many dreams I have in the video game industry, I am pursuing game development. I've had a passion for video games since the WII and DS were prominent. This interest evolved into my involvement in PC games. Throughout high school, I was forced away from video games in the interest of my studies, so instead of giving up video games, I decided to make my own. This way, playing video games would be a byproduct of my interest in making them. Though my passion for video games and the creation of them runs deeper than the restrictions placed on me in high school. My interest started at an earlier age when I realized how important video games were to me and my interest in helping others. I have many ideas that I hope to implement, even without the resources or a true plan.
