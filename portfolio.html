    Apps

    Team4Her

    Team4Her was a project I started working on last year, during the summer of my sophomore year. It was a project built for and during Technovation, a competition for apps and AI. A couple of friends and I got together and decided to build an app that could help solve a problem. Team4Her was an app built to help women and nonbinaries in STEM by providng them with more opportunities and better access to competitions such as Technovation. Not everyone has equal access to materials which puts them at an extreme disadvantage, so we are working to get rid of that.
    Built with Flutter

    Simply Make

    Simply Make was a project I also started summer of sophomore year. It was a project built for an entrepreneurship summer internship where we learned about building and marketing our product. Our app, Simply Make, was a project built for college students and busy adults that don't have time to go to the store to find ingredients for their recipes. They provide recipes based on the ingredients already in your fridge.
    Built with Flutter
